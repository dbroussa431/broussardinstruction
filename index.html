Certified to Protect & Educate

In 2017, I became a proud member of the United States Concealed Carry Association (USCCA) because I believed in their core mission: empowering legally armed citizens to protect themselves and their loved ones. I’ve always felt a calling to protect and educate those around me. In 2021, I became a certified instructor to fulfill that calling and to provide professional, responsible training to my community.
